The words fabric and cloth are used in textile assembly trades (such as tailoring and dressmaking) as synonyms for textile. however, there are subtle differences in these terms in specialized usage.Textile refers to any material made through weaving,knitting.Spreading ,crocheting or bonding that may be used in production of further goods (garments etc) cloth may be used synonymously with fabric but often refers to a finished piece of fabric used for a specific purpose.A fabric produced by weaving the warp yarns over several weft yarms at a time,creating a luster in silk nylon, and polyester materials
